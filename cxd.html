Georgie: Salesforce Chatbot Design Hackathon Winner

Won first place in the 2021 conversation chatbot design hackathon held by Salesforce. Created Georgie, a Georgetown events & activities conversation chatbot within 4 days in a hackathon along with two teammates, created a 200-line paper script, an intent & utterances file with 3 intents and 161 utterances, 1 welcoming path, 3 happy paths, 1 confusing path, and 1 edge case. Created a presentation deck and a demo for displaying functionality of the chatbot and presented in the celebration ceremony on May 7, 2021.

DMV Metro Google Action

The Google Action DMV Metro is a passion project that a Georgetown PhD student and I co-led a team of 5 for the Georgetown University Human Language Technology Group. It aims to provide Google Action users in the DMV area in the United States a quick way to obtain information on how long until the next metro approaches for a certain metro stop. Users can also have their home metro stops saved for quicker access.

Yumbasket Alexa Skill

The Alexa Skill Yumbasket is a project that I collaborated with 3 other designers in the conversation design course taught by Brielle Nickoloff in the LinguisticsCareerLaunch in 2021. It aims to help beginners to improve their cooking skills with both audio and visual aids.

Georgetown University Human Language Technology Group

I co-founded the GU-HLT group with Jungyoon Koh, a PhD student at Georgetown University to help connect people with background in humanities and computer science to collaborate on chatbot and voice app projects. We also hold bi-weekly speaker sessions and workshops on chatbot or voice app design and development.
