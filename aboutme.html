About Me

I am a computational linguist interested in the intersection of language, technology, and business. I am bilingual in Mandarin Chinese and English, and have knowledge in Korean, Modern Greek, and Spanish. In linguistics, I am mostly interested in automatic speech recognition, dialectology, syntax, and second language acquisition. I code in Python, C++, can do simple projects in Javascript, HTML and CSS. I also have knowledge in Linux and version control with Git.

I am currently working as a community manager at Botmock, a conversation design prototype product company. In the past, I have worked in translation, marketing, communications, social media management, and user operations. I have a set of creative skill sets including photography, videography, graphic design, and content design.

In my spare time, catch me singing, dancing hip-hop & K-pop choreography, playing piano & guitar, and playing League of Legends. I also love traveling and living in different places, and meeting all kinds of different people!
